Simultaneous Brightness Theory in UI Design

Simultaneous brightness theory plays a significant role in UI design as it influences the perception of colors and brightness. When two colors are placed side by side, their perceived brightness can change, affecting the overall visual appeal and readability of the interface. In UI design, it's essential to consider this phenomenon when selecting color schemes, backgrounds, and text colors to ensure a harmonious and visually pleasing user experience. By understanding simultaneous brightness theory, designers can create more effective and aesthetically appealing interfaces that cater to users' visual perception.
